Chicken Parmesan

Description

Chicken Parmesan is a classic Italian-American dish that consists of breaded chicken breast fried to a golden crisp, then topped with a rich marinara sauce and melted mozzarella and Parmesan cheese. It's a satisfying comfort food that is surprisingly easy to make at home.

This recipe ensures a perfectly crispy chicken cutlet with a flavorful sauce and gooey, bubbly cheese. It's a guaranteed crowd-pleaser for any weeknight dinner or special occasion.

Ingredients

  • 2 large chicken breasts, halved horizontally to make 4 cutlets
  • 1 cup Italian-style breadcrumbs
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 large eggs, beaten
  • 1/2 cup olive oil, for frying
  • 2 cups marinara sauce
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese

Steps

  1. Prepare the chicken cutlets. Place them between two sheets of plastic wrap and pound to an even 1/4-inch thickness.
  2. Set up a breading station with three shallow dishes. In the first, place the flour. In the second, the beaten eggs. In the third, mix the breadcrumbs, Parmesan cheese, salt, and pepper.
  3. Dredge each chicken cutlet first in the flour, then dip in the egg, and finally press firmly into the breadcrumb mixture to coat completely.
  4. Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Fry the chicken cutlets for about 3-4 minutes per side, until golden brown and cooked through. Remove and place on a wire rack to drain.
  5. Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C).
  6. Spoon a thin layer of marinara sauce in the bottom of a baking dish. Arrange the fried chicken on top.
  7. Spoon the remaining marinara sauce over the chicken, then sprinkle generously with the shredded mozzarella cheese.
  8. Bake for 15-20 minutes, or until the cheese is melted, bubbly, and slightly browned. Serve immediately.
