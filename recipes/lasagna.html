Lasagna

Description

This classic lasagna recipe is a family favorite that comes straight from my grandmother's kitchen. It's the perfect dish for a cozy Sunday dinner or for feeding a hungry crowd. The rich meat sauce, creamy cheese filling, and tender pasta layers combine to create a truly comforting and delicious meal.

While it takes a bit of time to assemble, every step is simple, and the results are absolutely worth it. Don't be afraid to make it your own by adding different cheeses or a pinch of red pepper flakes for some heat!

Ingredients

  • 1 pound sweet Italian sausage
  • 1 pound lean ground beef
  • 1 large white onion, minced
  • 5 cloves garlic, crushed
  • 1 (28 ounce) can crushed tomatoes
  • 2 (6.5 ounce) cans tomato sauce
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons dried basil leaves
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 (16 ounce) package lasagna noodles
  • 15 ounces ricotta cheese
  • 1 large egg
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 pound sliced mozzarella cheese

Steps

  1. In a large skillet, cook the sausage and ground beef over medium heat until well browned. Stir in the onion and garlic and cook until translucent. Drain off any excess grease.
  2. Stir in the crushed tomatoes, tomato sauce, water, and sugar. Season with basil and salt. Simmer, covered, for at least 1 hour, stirring occasionally.
  3. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Cook lasagna noodles for 8 to 10 minutes or until al dente. Drain and rinse with cold water.
  4. In a mixing bowl, combine ricotta cheese with the egg and half of the Parmesan cheese.
  5. Preheat your oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
  6. To assemble, spread 1 1/2 cups of the meat sauce in the bottom of a 9x13-inch baking dish. Arrange noodles lengthwise over the sauce. Spread with one-half of the ricotta cheese mixture. Top with a third of the mozzarella slices. Spoon 1 1/2 cups of meat sauce over mozzarella, and sprinkle with 1/4 cup Parmesan cheese.
  7. Repeat the layers, and top with the remaining mozzarella and Parmesan cheese. Cover with foil to prevent sticking.
  8. Bake in the preheated oven for 25 minutes. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 25 minutes until the top is bubbly and golden brown.
  9. Let the lasagna cool for at least 15 minutes before serving. This will help it set and make for cleaner slices.
